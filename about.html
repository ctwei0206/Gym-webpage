Things About This Gym

Why We Need to Work Out

Exercise improves heart health, reduces disease risk, boosts metabolism, and enhances mental wellbeing. Regular physical activity strengthens muscles and improves overall quality of life.

General Categories of Gym Workouts

Cardio
Improves heart rate and burns calories efficiently.
Weight Training
Builds muscle strength using resistance and weights.
Functional Training
Trains real-life movement patterns and improves mobility.
Stretching & Flexibility
Improves flexibility, posture, and reduces injury risk.
